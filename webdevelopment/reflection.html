Personal Reflection

Career and Course Reflection

Building this portfolio has helped me think more clearly about how my academic work, professional experience, and long-term career goals connect. My background is in public service and information technology, and this website gives me a place to organize that experience in a way that is easier for others to understand.

One of the main things I have learned through this course is the importance of planning before coding. It is easy to want to start building pages right away, but the wireframes, design plan, color choices, and navigation structure all helped me see how much thought goes into a usable website before the actual coding begins.

I have also learned that small details in HTML and CSS matter. A missing tag, incorrect file path, or poorly organized stylesheet can quickly affect how a page displays. Working through those issues has helped me become more comfortable troubleshooting and checking my work before submitting it.

My long-term career goal is to continue growing in information technology and eventually move into a director-level role. I enjoy leading teams, solving technical problems, and helping employees succeed. This portfolio supports that goal by giving me a professional space to show my education, technical projects, and career growth.

Stephen Bean | July 10, 2026
